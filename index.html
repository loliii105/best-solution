About XonTel

A leading provider of smart communications and advanced business systems since 2004. Based in Kuwait, we deliver reliable, AI-powered solutions including unified communications, contact centers, cloud integration, smart home integration, and IoT security systems to enterprises and government entities across the Middle East. Proud owner and developer of the TelSip Communications Network, we are an IEEE member and CE certified.

XonTel delivers cutting-edge communication technologies tailored for modern businesses

XonTel System Solutions

  • XonTel PBX Manager: A powerful telephony platform offering flexible, secure, and cost-effective voice communication.
  • XonTel Call Center: A complete omnichannel customer service suite with real-time monitoring, and smart routing.
  • XonTel AI: AI-enhanced tools that automate interactions, analyze customer sentiment, and boost productivity.
  • XonTel Mobile App: A feature-rich mobile solution that enables seamless communication and business management on the go.
